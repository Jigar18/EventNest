Description -

Hello Coders!
HackwithIndia is more than simply a hackathon; it is a showcase of human potential, cooperation, and technology's revolutionary power. In a world full with obstacles, events like these serve as beacons of hope, reminding us that by using the power of invention and collaborating, we can overcome any obstacle and create a brighter
Everyone, from experienced coders to first-time hackers, is welcome to participate in this unique meeting of talent. Whether you're a coding genius, a design virtuoso, or simply full of ideas, the Indie's Biggest Hackathon provides a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere in which everyone may succeed.

Details -

The event will be 48 hours long and will take place on a website we will design and host.

Participants must register their teams on this website.

Participants are allowed to use anything they want during the event; there are no restrictions to what participants can do to get points except for helping other teams in any form (offline or online), or DDoS attacking the event platform.

We hope to make this a gentle practical introduction to the world of CTFs for those who may not have participated in any before and a fun yet challenging event for experienced people as well.
